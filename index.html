My Works

Guessing Game

This simple guessing game where the player tries to guess three random numbers generated by the computer. The numbers are between 1 and 10, and the player has a limited number of attempts to guess all three correctly.

Jak En Poy

Jak En Poy is a classic rock, paper, scissors game where the player competes against the computer. The player chooses between rock, paper, or scissors, and the computer randomly selects its choice. The game interface displays the current score and a message indicating whether the player won, lost, or tied each round.

In Between Cards

In Between Cards is an engaging card game where the player aims to guess if a drawn card falls between two randomly selected numbers. The game begins with the computer generating two numbers, and the player is dealt a card from a deck. The game includes options for different betting amounts and win conditions, adding excitement and strategy to each round.
